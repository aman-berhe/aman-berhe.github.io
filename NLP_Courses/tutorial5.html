Stemming and Lemmatization

Stemming and lemmatization are techniques used to reduce words to their base or root form.

Python Example: Stemming and Lemmatization


from nltk.stem import PorterStemmer, WordNetLemmatizer
nltk.download('wordnet')

words = ["running", "flies", "happily"]
stemmer = PorterStemmer()
lemmatizer = WordNetLemmatizer()

stems = [stemmer.stem(word) for word in words]
lemmas = [lemmatizer.lemmatize(word, pos='v') for word in words]

print("Stems:", stems)
print("Lemmas:", lemmas)

Output: Stems: ['run', 'fli', 'happili']

Output: Lemmas: ['run', 'fly', 'happily']
